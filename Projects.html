    LED Cube - 1728 Voxel

    This projects consists of a large 12 x 12 x 12 matrix of 5mm green LEDs that are individually adressible. This setup will allow the animation of monochromatic images in 3D space. The cube is controlled by a large line of shift reisters that turn the gates on of the drive transistors, to turn on the layer and LED column to be iluminated. One layer is iluminated at a time, with a fast refresh rate in order to utilize the effects of persistance of vision.
